About ColorAid Charts

Making data visualization accessible to everyone

Our Mission

At ColorAid Charts, we believe accessibility is a fundamental right. Our goal is to create colorblind-friendly charts that use contrast, patterns, and clarity to make data universally understandable.

How We Work

We combine design best practices with modern web technologies to ensure inclusivity in visual communication.
